**The mental model I'd suggest**

Think of your life in three layers:

**Layer 1 — Identity** (permanent, never changes) Who you're becoming. Your goals list. This just sits there. You look at it occasionally, you don't interact with it daily.

**Layer 2 — Rhythms** (weekly structure) Not a schedule — a rhythm. What happens every morning, every evening, every week. Simple enough to survive exam season on 20% effort.

**Layer 3 — Focus** (monthly/quarterly) One or two things getting real attention this season. Everything else is just maintenance.

The insight is: **Layer 1 and 2 never break.** Only Layer 3 pauses during exams. So you never feel like you've "lost the system" — you just downshifted.
